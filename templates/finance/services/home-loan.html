Eligibility Criteria

  • You need to be at least 21 years of age to buy a property
  • Maximum age to get a home loan is 60 years if you are salaried and 65 years if you are self-employed but this can change according to the lender.
  • You need to have a steady income to be able to get a mortgage loan as your monthly income alone can help determine your loan amount.
  • Credit bureau score: Existing credit history is preferred, though not mandatory.
