Overview

Machinery Loan is a type of loan that is used to purchase machinery and equipment. It is a popular option for businesses that need to purchase machinery to improve their operations. Let us help you get the best machinery loan deals with guidance from our experts.

Eligibility Criteria

  • Minimum of 1 year of business operation for businesses applying.
  • Active income source (salary, business income, etc.).
  • No EMI bounces in the past 12 months.
  • A good credit history is preferred but not mandatory.

Mandatory Requirements

  • Valid PAN Card, Aadhar Card, Passport-size photo, and Latest Address Proof.
  • Business Proof (GST Registration, Udyam Aadhaar, or Shop & Establishment Certificate).
  • Financial Proof: Last two years of financial statements and any loan sanction letters.
