Overview

Working capital is a financial product offered by banks and non-banking financial companies (NBFCs), which allows an individual to withdraw more money than their current account balance, up to a pre-approved limit. This facility helps manage cash flow issues and acts as a short-term financing option. Finance Mandi, as a channel partner with banks and NBFCs, assists customers in availing the best deals for overdraft facilities.
