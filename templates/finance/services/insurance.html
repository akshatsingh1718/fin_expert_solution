Overview

Insurance is a financial product that provides protection against various risks and uncertainties. It involves pooling resources from policyholders to create a fund that can be used to compensate individuals or entities in case of losses or damages. {{ COMPANY_NAME }} being a leading channel partner with Banks and NBFCs, can help you secure the best insurance deals tailored to your financial needs.

Eligibility Criteria

  • You must be at least 18 years old to apply for insurance.
  • The car should not be older than 10 years, though this may vary based on the lender.
  • You need to have a stable income to qualify, whether salaried or self-employed.
  • A good credit score enhances the chances of approval, but it's not always mandatory.

Mandatory Requirements

Salaried and Self-Employed Basic documents Individual and company document as per applicable lists

  • PAN Card, Passport, Voter ID, or Aadhaar Card.
  • Recent utility bill (electricity, water, gas) or rent agreement.
  • Registration Certificate (RC) of the car.
  • Salary slips for the last 3 months and bank statements for the last 6 months.
  • Business income proof, bank statements for the last 12 months, and tax returns.
