Overview

To get a loan at low interest rates, to meet your financial needs for emergencies, education etc mortgaging existing property is a great way. This will allow you to meet your financial needs with ease through LAP, without actually selling the same. We help you get flexible repayment options by NBFCs and Banks and help you avail best deals for loans. Loan against property enables mortgaging of residential and commercial properties at attractive rates for quick funds. Both residential and commercial properties* are accepted as collateral, and the money from the {{ COMPANY_NAME }} Loan Against Property can be used for a variety of personal or business needs.

Eligibility Criteria

  • You need to be at least 21 years of age to mortgage a property.
  • Maximum age to get a home loan is 60 years if you are salaried and 65 years if you are self-employed but this can change according to the lender.
  • You need to have a steady income to be able to get a mortgage loan as your monthly income alone can help determine your loan amount
  • Credit bureau score: Existing credit history is preferred, though not mandatory.

Mandatory Requirements

Salaried and Self-Employed Basic documents Individual and company document as per applicable lists

  • Utility bill as proof of residence
  • Statement of account from your bank
  • Salary Proof (Salary certificate / Pay slip)
  • Business income proof (for businessman)
  • Chain agreements of the said property
