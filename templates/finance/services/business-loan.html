Overview

It is an unsecured business loan taken by an Individual, Proprietary, Partnership & LLP firms or entity from a bank or a non-banking financial company (NBFC) on the basis of his business income and banking to meet their business financial needs. The {{ COMPANY_NAME }} is one of the leading channel partners with Banks and NBFC and can help you avail unsecured business loans. We help you procure the best deals for your loan needs.

Eligibility Criteria

  • Company: A company should be minimum 1 yr old.
  • Business activity: There should be a business activity set-up and stock.
  • Company: There should be an active company current account with Min 15k ABB (Average bank balance).
  • Credit bureau score: Existing credit history is preferred, though not mandatory.

Mandatory Requirements

Individual Document

  • Pan Card, Passport size photo & Adhar card of Applicant and Co-Applicant
  • Latest current residence address proof in name of Applicant or family members with relation proof

Company Document

  • Pan card for Partnership, LLP firm, PVT Ltd & LTD company only
  • MOA & AOA for PVT Ltd & LTD company only
  • Register partnership deed for Partnership firm and LLP firm only
  • Business legal proof (Gst Registration Certificate, Udhayam aadhar Certificate and Shop & Establishment)
  • Business vintage proof
  • Last two years Complete set of financial Existing Secured and unsecured any loan sanction letters
  • Last one-year Business primary Current or saving account
